Worth every mile between us

Dave and Eunike's lives intertwined when they met at Pensacola Christian College. What started as a friendship soon blossomed into a deep and meaningful connection. In 2018, they embarked on a romantic journey, discovering a love that would transcend time. Despite the challenges of a long-distance relationship that followed, Dave and Eunike persevered with unwavering commitment and a shared vision of their future together.


The miles between them only fueled their love, as they navigated the complexities of distance with trust, open communication, and a firm belief in their love's strength. Their story is a testament to the power of love conquering all obstacles, proving that when two souls are destined to be together, no distance can keep them apart.
