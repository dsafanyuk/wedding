Wow! Indonesia is far, will you hate us if we can't make it?

Absolutely not! We totally understand if you cannot make the long flight to Indonesia! We are still considering a US wedding/reception once our immigration procedure is completed. We will keep you all updated.

How do we get to Indonesia?

Your best bet would be to fly to CGK (Jakarta airport code). You can take a boat or swim, but we do not recommend it and cannot be held accountable for any brave people who decide to take that route.

This will be our first time in Indonesia, what can we expect?

Welcome! If you are planning to come, please do us a favor and RSVP ASAP. We totally understand if this is short notice for some people and cannot make it. After you confirm your attendance, Dave can reach out to help make arrangements (Flights/hotels/transport)

Will there be any provided accommodations/transportation?

At this time, there will not be any provided accommodations. However, there are definitely hotels that can fit your budget/preferences. For transportation, please reach out to us directly to help organize transportation to/from the airport (CGK airport code)
